    ★★★★★ 4 years ago

    I'm 51 years old, and had a total hip replacement My doctor said I would need home health and home health PT for six weeks after my surgery. I am not from this area, so I knew nothing about any of the home health agencies in the area. Elite was listed as being in network with my insurance provider. I have to admit that when I saw the three stars and some of their reviews, I was a bit worried about what type of care I would receive. I have been in healthcare for over 25 years, so I know what to look for care wise, etc. I was very pleased with my services that I received from Elite. The nurses gave exceptional care, showed up as scheduled, were professional and friendly. They were also very thorough and answered any questions or concerns that I had. In addition to my scheduled visits, nursing staff from their office called and checked on me weekly to make sure I was okay and that I wasn't having any issues with their services. I definitely recommend their services to anyone needing home health care.
